Projects

A collection of some of my favorite projects. If you're interested, some of the images link to additional information!

Salt E: Salt Sprinkling Robot

A bluetooth-controlled robot that sprinkles salt on icy driveways.

Roy G. Biv's Color Drop

A colorful carnival game that combines the mechanisms of foosball and Plinko.

Stitch Loop

A mobile application that combines habit tracking, closet organization, and social networking to promote circularity in fashion.

Fren

A social event-finding mobile application for users to join spontaneous events with new people.

Raindrop Sonata

A design for a collection of rain-activated collectibles that encourage users to enjoy rainy days.

Kinevix

A two player "tactile foosball" board game that considers the interaction between players.

AllePal

A design for a night-time stuffed animal shaped air purifier that relieves allergies.

Steve the Skeleton

An interactive Halloween decoration using an ultrasonic distance sensor, servo motor, and led lights.
