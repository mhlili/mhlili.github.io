Hello there!

My name is Maggie Lee. I am a second year at Northeastern University studying Computer Engineering and Interaction Design.

I'm driven by products made with intention. Therefore, I put immense effort and thoughtfulness into everything I create and take part in. To implement my ideas without limitations on technical ability, I work hard to be a quick learner. Over the years, I have developed strong programming skills, particularly in Java and HTML, and more working knowledge in C++, Python, and Kotlin. I am also proficient in design software including Figma, AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Adobe InDesign, and Adobe Illustrator. More recently, I have become involved in creating more physical projects, where I have developed skills in Arduino, laser cutting, soldering, and general prototype fabrication.
