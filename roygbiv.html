Roy G. Biv's Color Drop

A color-filled carnival game that combines the mechanisms of Plinko and Foosball.

About

Roy G. Biv Color Drop requires two players. Each player picks a board and assembles the pieces at the top of the board. The objective is to get the ball in the center bin and score the most points. However, once the pieces are placed, players drop the ball down from the top of the board and the other player attempts to block it from the middle.
